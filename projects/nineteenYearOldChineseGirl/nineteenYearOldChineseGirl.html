I Am Friends With ...Lisha is a series of stickers, hoodies and t-shirts I designed and screenprinted as holiday gag gifts in 2018. The design reads "I am friends with a 19-year-old Chinese girl named Lisha." I printed over 50 t-shirts/sweatshirts and 100 stickers in multiple runs over the entirety of the season. I turned twenty a few months later.

In 2022, I printed and sold 80 new shirts with a new graphic and new gender that reads "I am friends with a 23-year-old Chinese non-binary person named Lisha". Next time, I'll add my political afflication, sexual orientation or tax returns!
