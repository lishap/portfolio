I Am Friends With A 19 Year Old Chinese Girl Named Lisha is a series of stickers, hoodies and t-shirts I designed and screenprinted as holiday gag gifts in 2018.

I printed over 50 t-shirts/sweatshirts and 100 stickers in multiple runs over the entirety of the season. I turned twenty a few months later. I plan on doing an anniverary "I Am Friends With a 23 Year Old Chinese non-binary person named Lisha" edition soon.
