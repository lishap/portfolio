Gobyworld is an absurd point-and-click adventure, where you help Goby, a piece of swallowed gum, in his quest to find freedom beyond the digestive system.

Initially, Gobyworld was my B.F.A. capstone project but was completed at The Rabbit 2023 Games Residency supported by Medienboard Berlin-Brandenburg. Gobyworld was presented at Berlin's Amaze Fest Open Screens 2023.
