Song Lookback is a web application that captures your portrait including your favorite song in a given month using p5.js and Spotify API. Song Lookback uses p5.js and clmTracker to capture the user's face. It uses Spotify API to get the user's top song of the month, and displays this on their head. Future iterations will include a toggleable disco mode, proper photo booth and scapbook gallery.
