What2Watch is UX/UI design for a web application that chooses what to watch for you.

For online media consumers, it’s a constant challenge to pick what to watch. Each streaming service requires you to log in, and then users must search at length to decide on something to watch. This is especially true if you’re a subscriber to multiple streaming services. The design is attempting to thwart ‘paradox of choice’.

After competitively analyzing Netflix, Hulu, HBO, Amazon Prime Video, JustWatch, ReelGood, NYTimes Watching, Find A Movie, Decider, Movie-Map and Criterion Channel, it was clear there was no simple app. What2Watch beats the competitive landscape by not requiring login or account setup, connecting directly to streaming services and simple design.
