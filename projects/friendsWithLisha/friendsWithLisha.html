I Am Friends With ...Lisha is a series of stickers, t-shirts and hoodies designed and hand screenprinted. In 2018, the first run "I am friends with a 19 year old Chinese girl named Lisha" were done as holiday gag gifts. I burned my own screen and printed on whatever extra blank shirts I could find. I printed over 50 t-shirts/sweatshirts and 100 stickers in multiple runs over the entirety of the season. I turned twenty a few months later.

In 2022, I hand screenprinted and sold 80 new shirts with both a new graphics, colors and gender. I custom ordered baby pink and red American union made 5.5 oz cotton jersey blanks. They read "I am friends with a 23-year-old Chinese non-binary person named Lisha". My buddy 'RX Skulls' screenprinted the stickers on colorful eggshell blanks. Next time, I'll add my political afflication, sexual orientation and tax returns!
